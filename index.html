In one breath

What the EU AI Act is

The world's first law for AI. It does not ban AI. It ranks AI by risk, and the higher the risk, the more rules you follow. That is the whole idea.

The four ranks

How it sorts AI

UnacceptableBanned. Social scoring, manipulation, reading emotions at work.
High riskStrict rules. Hiring, credit, health, education, policing.
LimitedJust be open. Chatbots and AI content must say they are AI.
MinimalFree to use. Spam filters, game AI, everyday tools.

The whole job is knowing which rank you are in. That is where I come in.
